Fifteen Puzzle

The goal of the fifteen puzzle is to un-jumble its fifteen squares by repeatedly making moves that slide squares into the empty space. How quickly can you solve it?

American puzzle author and mathmatician Sam Loyd is often falsely credited with creading the puzzle; indeed, Loyd claimed from 1891 until his death in 1911 tha he invented it. The puzzle was actualy created around 1874 by Noyes Palmer Chapman, a postmaster in Canastota, New York.
